Mamas HomeMade Lemon Meringue Pie





One 9-inch pie
30 Min-Prep time
10 Min-Cook time
40 Min-Total
8 Servings





Ingredients:

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 3 table spoons cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 2 lemons,juiced and zested
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4 egg yolks ,beaten
  • 1(9inch)pie crust,baked
  • 4 egg whites
  • 6 tablespoons white sugar

Directions:

  1. Preheat Oven:Preheat oven to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C)
  2. Make Lemon Filling:In a medium saucepan...
    1. Whisk together 1 cup sugar,flour,cornsnatch and salt
    2. Stir in water,lemon juice and lemon zest
    3. Cook over medium-high heat, stirring frequently,until mixture comes to a boil
    4. Stir in butter
    5. Place egg yolks in a small bowl and grdually whisk in 1/2 cup of hot sugar mixture
    6. Whisk egg yolk mixture back into remaining sugar mixture
    7. Bring to a boil and continue to cook while stirring constantly until thick
    8. Remove from heat
    9. Pour filling into baked pastry shell
  3. Make Meringue: In a large glass or metal bowl...
    1. Whip egg whites until foamy
    2. Add sugar gradually,and continue to whip until stiff peaks from
    3. Spread meringue over pie, sealing the edges at the crust
  4. Bake: Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes, or until meringue is golden brown.
